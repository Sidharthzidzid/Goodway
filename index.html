About Goodway Maritime

Established in 2019, Goodway Marine Training and Services stands as a beacon of guidance for maritime enthusiasts with a track record of successfully steering 180 students across 7 batches towards rewarding careers.

We pride ourselves on offering specialized services such as personalized pre-sea counseling and comprehensive support for IMU entrance examinations. At Goodway, we simplify the journey to a maritime career by handling all documentation, including medical clearance.

Our commitment extends beyond training, as we actively provide placement assistance, connecting our students with promising opportunities in the maritime industry.

Our Mission

"At Goodway Marine Training & Services, our mission is to foster complete awareness about the marine industry and emphasize the significance of marine courses and careers. We are dedicated to guiding students in selecting the most suitable course based on their educational qualifications, physical fitness, financial stability, and age."

Admission Procedure

Follow these steps to begin your maritime journey with Goodway

1

Application Submission

Submit the application form along with the following documents:

  • Copy of mark sheet of 10th/+2/others
  • Copy of Passport and Aadhar
  • Medical Certificate
  • Latest Passport size color photographs (4 nos) with a white background
  • Duly signed application by Parent/Guardian of the candidate
  • Course registration fee of Rs. 25,000/- (non-refundable)
2

Selection Process

Candidates will be selected based on:

  • Merit basis in entrance tests
  • Interview (in-person, phone, or zoom interview)
  • Good spoken English skills

All candidates must attend the IMU entrance exam for long-term courses (one year and above).

3

Eligibility Criteria

Physical, Medical, and Mental Fitness:

  • Candidates must be physically, medically, and mentally fit for sea services according to standard norms
  • Eyesight should be 6/6 with no color blindness
  • Provide a certified medical certificate from a DG Shipping approved doctor

Educational Qualifications:

  • Candidates should have passed the 10th standard or its equivalent from a recognized board
  • Minimum 40% aggregate in class X
  • At least 40% in English in 10th or 12th standards

Rules and Regulations

  • The use of alcohol, tobacco, or intoxicants in any form on the campus/institution is strictly prohibited.
  • The usage of mobile phones on the campus during the training time is strictly prohibited.
  • No candidate is permitted to leave the academy without prior permission.
  • Students should attend classes punctually. The attendance required for appearing in the final examination is 95%.
  • All fees, once paid, are non-refundable, both in part or in full.
  • The management has the right to take any kind of action against candidates in case of a violation of the academy's rules.
